## Connections

### Voltage Sensor Module → ESP32

| Voltage Sensor Pin | ESP32 Pin |
|-------------------|-----------|
| VCC | 3.3V |
| GND | GND |
| OUT | GPIO34 |

### I2C LCD (16x2) → ESP32

| LCD Pin | ESP32 Pin |
|----------|-----------|
| VCC | 5V |
| GND | GND |
| SDA | GPIO21 |
| SCL | GPIO22 |

## Complete System Connections

```text
Solar Panel
      ↓
Charge Controller
      ↓
Rechargeable Battery
      ↓
Wireless Transmitter Coil
      ↓
Wireless Power Transfer
      ↓
Receiver Coil
      ↓
Rectifier Circuit
      ↓
EV Battery / DC Load

Voltage Sensor OUT  ---> GPIO34 (ESP32)
Voltage Sensor VCC  ---> 3.3V
Voltage Sensor GND  ---> GND

LCD SDA             ---> GPIO21
LCD SCL             ---> GPIO22
LCD VCC             ---> 5V
LCD GND             ---> GND
```
